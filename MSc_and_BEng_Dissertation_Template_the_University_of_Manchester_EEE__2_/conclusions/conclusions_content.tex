\subsection{Conclusions}

This dissertation addressed the ``SF Monoculture'' --- the congestion collapse that occurs
when standard path-planning heuristics ignore LoRaWAN protocol dynamics. The central thesis
was that treating the UAV's physical position as a direct control input for the radio
protocol would enable a DRL agent to actively resolve this bottleneck. The work demonstrates
that this thesis is correct.

\subsubsection{Summary of Contributions}

Five contributions are delivered. (1)~\textbf{Simulation environment}: the first open framework modelling the complete causal chain from UAV position through RSSI, EMA-ADR convergence, and Capture Effect to packet delivery in a single Gymnasium step, with Two-Ray path loss, log-normal shadowing ($\sigma=4$\,dB), and asynchronous 10\% duty cycles. (2)~\textbf{Curriculum learning}: a three-stage domain-randomisation curriculum producing a single DQN that generalises across a 100$\times$ area variation and four-fold density range without retraining. (3)~\textbf{Multi-objective reward}: AoI urgency, buffer-overflow penalties on all action types, a discovery bonus, and an energy term prevent indefinite hovering over high-rate sensors. (4)~\textbf{SF-Aware Greedy baseline}: a novel protocol-aware comparator that isolates long-horizon planning gain from protocol awareness alone. (5)~\textbf{HIL readiness profile}: five orthogonal perturbation tests confirm robustness to GPS noise, path-loss shifts, wind drift, and observation latency; duty-cycle tightening below 6\% is the primary physical sensitivity.

\subsubsection{Key Findings}

All three agents achieve 100\% sensor coverage in all conditions. The DQN's throughput
advantage grows with sensor count: $+2.3\%$ over SF-Aware Greedy at $N=10$ ($p<0.001$,
$d=2.52$), rising to $+9.0\%$ at $N=30$ and $+8.6\%$ at $N=40$ ($d=0.54$, $p=0.043$),
significant at all four tested densities; energy efficiency improves by up to $+8.7\%$.
Fairness improves by $+4.2\%$ at $N=40$ and is retained at $1{,}000\times1{,}000$ scale
($\mathcal{J}=0.897$ vs.\ $0.893$) even where throughput is indistinguishable ($p=0.96$).

\subsubsection{Objectives Revisited}

All six project objectives were fully achieved. The custom Gymnasium environment (Obj.~1--2)
models the complete causal chain from UAV position through EMA-ADR convergence and Capture
Effect to packet delivery. The MDP formulation (Obj.~3) was empirically calibrated so no
reward component exceeded 15\% of total episode reward. The trained agent exhibits emergent
deliberate repositioning (2.9\% of steps, $p=0.002$, $d=2.1$), yielding $+14\%$ bytes per
collection step (Obj.~4), with statistical significance confirmed at all four network densities
(Obj.~5) and duty-cycle tightening below 6\% identified as the primary Sim-to-Real sensitivity
(Obj.~6).
