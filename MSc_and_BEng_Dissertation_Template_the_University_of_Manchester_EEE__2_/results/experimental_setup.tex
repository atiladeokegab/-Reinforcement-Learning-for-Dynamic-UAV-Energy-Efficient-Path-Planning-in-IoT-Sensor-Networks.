\subsection{Experimental Setup}
\label{sec:exp_setup}

All agents are evaluated on the Gymnasium simulation
(Section~\ref{sec:env}): UAV at 100\,m altitude, 10\,m per grid cell,
274\,Wh battery, 2100-step episode. Both learned policies use
deterministic action selection (DQN: $\varepsilon{=}0$, frame stack
$k{=}4$; Relational-RL: $\arg\max$ over $\pi_\theta$). The scalability
sweep covers five matched conditions from $(100{\times}100, N{=}10)$ to
$(500{\times}500, N{=}50)$, holding sensor density at one per
$10^3$ cells. Each condition uses \textbf{50 independent seeds per
agent}, sharing identical seed-controlled layouts for paired analysis.
A separate 20-seed architectural diagnostic at $(200{\times}200,
N{=}20)$ adds boundary-cell occupancy and wall-collision counters
(Section~\ref{sec:diagnostic}). Per-episode metrics are cumulative
reward, NDR, Jain's fairness index, and energy efficiency (bytes/Wh).
Significance uses Welch's $t$-test (two-sided, $\alpha{=}0.05$) and
bootstrap CIs ($10{,}000$ resamples) on per-seed deltas.

Four baselines are evaluated alongside the two learned policies:
Nearest-Sensor Greedy (nearest buffered sensor); SF-Aware
Max-Throughput Greedy (battery-adaptive scoring over SF quality, buffer
fill, and distance); TSP Oracle via Nearest-Neighbour~$+$~2-opt
(Algorithm~\ref{alg:tsp},~\cite{lin1973effective}) as a routing-optimal
upper bound; and Lawnmower at 5 seeds as an indicative coverage
reference (Appendix~\ref{app:lawnmower}).
