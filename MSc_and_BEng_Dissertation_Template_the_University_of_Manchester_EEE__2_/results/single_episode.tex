\subsection{Single-Episode Trajectory Analysis}

Figure~\ref{fig:single_episode} shows a representative evaluation episode for the trained
DQN agent on the 20-sensor, 500$\times$500 condition. The agent achieves 100\% sensor
coverage by step~200 and sustains data collection until battery depletion at step~1{,}404,
collecting 57{,}335\,bytes at a mean efficiency of 213.6\,B/Wh. Critically, the cumulative
reward grows monotonically throughout the episode without plateauing, indicating that the
agent does not idle once early sensors are visited. This behaviour is a direct consequence
of the terminal unvisited-sensor penalty ($-2{,}000$ per sensor) and the per-step data
reward encouraging continued collection from revisited sensors.
