This chapter evaluates the trained flat-MLP DQN against heuristic
and oracle baselines, diagnoses its learned behaviour, and then
(Chapter~\ref{sec:relational}) evaluates the permutation-invariant
Relational-RL policy against the same suite. All comparisons use 50
seeds per condition where feasible; diagnostic and indicative
comparisons are flagged explicitly. Sections cover the experimental
setup (\ref{sec:exp_setup}), a single-episode qualitative look
(\ref{sec:single_episode}), the 50-seed multi-condition
benchmark (\ref{sec:multi_seed}), the operational-envelope sweep
(\ref{sec:scalability}), the architectural diagnostic that motivates
the second results chapter (\ref{sec:diagnostic}), the component
ablation (\ref{sec:ablation}), the sim-to-real robustness sweep
(\ref{sec:robustness}), and a discussion of mechanisms and
limitations (\ref{sec:discussion}).
