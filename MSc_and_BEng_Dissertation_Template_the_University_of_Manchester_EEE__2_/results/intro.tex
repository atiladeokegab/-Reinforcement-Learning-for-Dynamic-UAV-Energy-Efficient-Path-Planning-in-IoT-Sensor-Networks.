\subsection{Introduction}

This chapter evaluates the performance of the proposed protocol-aware Deep Reinforcement
Learning (DRL) agent. To validate the framework, we conduct a comparative analysis against
two standard path-planning heuristics: a Nearest Neighbour approach and a sophisticated
SF-Aware Max-Throughput strategy. The performance is quantified in terms of data collection
efficiency, network coverage, and energy consumption within the high-fidelity Gymnasium
simulation.
