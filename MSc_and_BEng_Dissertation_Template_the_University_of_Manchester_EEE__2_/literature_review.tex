\section{Literature Review} \label{sec:lit}

UAVs extend data collection to remote and hazardous environments where static infrastructure
is impractical. Wei et al.~\cite{wei2022uav} survey UAV-assisted data collection and
identify three structural advantages over fixed gateways: flexible deployment, line-of-sight
communication links, and on-demand servicing of sparse sensor networks. The transition from
static to mobile gateways, however, introduces complex challenges in network management and
protocol stability.
