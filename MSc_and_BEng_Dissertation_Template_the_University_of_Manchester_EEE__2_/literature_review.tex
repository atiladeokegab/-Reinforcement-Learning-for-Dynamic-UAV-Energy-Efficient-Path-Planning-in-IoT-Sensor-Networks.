\section{Literature Review} \label{sec:lit}

The integration of Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) into Internet of Things (IoT) ecosystems
has revolutionised data collection in remote and hazardous environments. As detailed in the
comprehensive survey by Wei et al.~\cite{wei2022uav}, UAV-assisted data collection offers
distinct advantages over static infrastructure, including flexible deployment, line-of-sight
communication links, and the ability to service sparse sensor networks on demand. However,
the transition from static to mobile gateways introduces complex challenges in network
management and protocol stability.
