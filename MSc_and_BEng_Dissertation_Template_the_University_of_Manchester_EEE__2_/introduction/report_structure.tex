\subsection{Report structure}

This report is organised as follows. \autoref{sec:lit} reviews UAV-assisted data collection,
LoRaWAN scalability, and DRL in wireless networks. \autoref{sec:prelim} covers prerequisite
concepts: the LoRaWAN physical layer, Two-Ray channel model, and Q-Learning fundamentals.
\autoref{sec:method} presents the system model, Gymnasium environment, and DRL formulation.
\autoref{sec:results} reports multi-seed comparative performance, ablation studies, robustness
analysis, and a discussion of mechanisms. Conclusions and future work are in
\autoref{sec:conclusions}.
