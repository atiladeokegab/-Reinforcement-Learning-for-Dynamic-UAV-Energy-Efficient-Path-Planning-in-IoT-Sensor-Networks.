\subsection{Report structure}

\autoref{sec:lit} surveys UAV-assisted data collection, LoRaWAN scalability, deep RL for path planning, and permutation-invariant policy architectures. \autoref{sec:prelim} covers the prerequisite concepts. \autoref{sec:method} describes the simulation environment, MDP formulation, reward function, domain-randomisation curriculum, and both learned policies alongside the baseline agents.

\autoref{sec:results} evaluates the flat-MLP DQN through multi-seed benchmarks, a component ablation, and the architectural diagnostic identifying its representational ceiling. \autoref{sec:relational} presents the permutation-invariant Relational-RL policy and its head-to-head comparison with the DQN. Conclusions and future work are in \autoref{sec:conclusions}.
