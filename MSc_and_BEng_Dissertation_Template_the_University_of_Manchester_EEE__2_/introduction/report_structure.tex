\subsection{Report structure}

This report is organised as follows. \autoref{sec:lit} reviews existing literature on
UAV-assisted data collection, LoRaWAN scalability challenges, and the application of Deep
Reinforcement Learning (DRL) in wireless networks. \autoref{sec:prelim} discusses preliminary
concepts fundamental to this work, including the LoRaWAN physical layer, the Two-Ray Ground
Reflection model, and the fundamentals of Q-Learning.

The project's methodology is detailed in \autoref{sec:method}, which presents the system
model, the custom Gymnasium simulation environment, and the formulation of the DRL agent's
state space and reward function. The simulation results and a critical discussion of the
agent's performance against greedy baselines are presented in \autoref{sec:results}. Finally,
the conclusion and a consideration of future research avenues---specifically regarding
generalisation and the ``Sim-to-Real'' gap---can be found in \autoref{sec:conclusions}.
