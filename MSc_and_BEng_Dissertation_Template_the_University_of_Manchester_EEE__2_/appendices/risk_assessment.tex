\section{Risk Register and Health \& Safety Risk Assessment}
\label{app:risk}

The following table identifies the principal risks associated with this project, together
with their likelihood, potential impact, and the mitigation strategies employed.

\begin{table}[H]
\centering
\caption{Project Risk Register}
\label{tab:risk}
\begin{tabularx}{\textwidth}{p{3cm} p{1.5cm} p{1.5cm} X}
\toprule
\textbf{Risk} & \textbf{Likelihood} & \textbf{Impact} & \textbf{Mitigation} \\
\midrule
\textbf{Simulation instability}: The custom Gymnasium environment contains bugs
that cause non-physical state transitions or incorrect reward computation.
& Medium & High &
Extensive unit testing of each environment component (propagation model, ADR logic,
Capture Effect) in isolation before integration. Episode step counters and boundary
clipping enforce physically plausible states. \\
\midrule
\textbf{Training divergence}: The DQN agent fails to converge due to reward
scale mismatch, exploding gradients, or excessive exploration.
& Medium & High &
Reward normalisation and clipping; gradient clipping; target network stabilisation;
hyperparameter sweep on learning rate and batch size. \\
\midrule
\textbf{Overfitting to training scenarios}: The trained policy memorises fixed
sensor layouts and fails to generalise to unseen configurations.
& High & High &
Domain Randomisation: sensor positions, grid sizes, and sensor counts are randomised
every episode during training. Curriculum Learning ensures gradual exposure to
increasing difficulty. \\
\midrule
\textbf{Computational resource constraints}: Training requires GPU resources
not available on personal hardware.
& Low & Medium &
Primary training conducted on NVIDIA RTX 3050 Ti GPU (personal laptop). Long training
runs ($2 \times 10^6$ steps) scheduled overnight. Stable-Baselines3 used for
efficient GPU-accelerated training. \\
\midrule
\textbf{Sim-to-Real gap}: The trained policy performs well in simulation but
fails to transfer to a physical UAV due to unmodelled dynamics.
& Medium & Medium &
The simulation incorporates high-fidelity models (Two-Ray, log-normal shadowing,
EMA-ADR) specifically to reduce this gap. Domain Randomisation further improves
robustness. Acknowledged as a limitation; HIL validation proposed as future work. \\
\midrule
\textbf{Time overrun}: Experimental evaluation and dissertation write-up take
longer than planned.
& Medium & Medium &
Modular dissertation structure with individual \texttt{.tex} files per section allows
parallel writing and incremental compilation. Regular supervisor meetings to track
progress and adjust scope if necessary. \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabularx}
\end{table}

\subsection*{Health \& Safety Risk Assessment}

materials, human participants, or off-campus fieldwork. The following table constitutes
the formal H\&S Risk Assessment, completed in accordance with the university's online
health and safety induction.

\noindent\textbf{Overall risk level:} Low. No hazardous materials, fieldwork, human
participants, or physical experiments are involved. No special permits or ethical approval
are required.
