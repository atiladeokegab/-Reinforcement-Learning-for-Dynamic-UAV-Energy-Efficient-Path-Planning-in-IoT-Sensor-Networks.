\subsection{Project Management}
\label{sec:project_management}

The project followed a six-milestone Gantt-based plan (Appendix~\ref{app:gantt}), front-loading environment design and DQN training into Semester~1 to surface modelling issues before committing GPU time to evaluation. Weekly meetings with Dr.~Zahra Mobini served as progress checkpoints, with the plan updated at each semester boundary.

Two deviations from the original plan occurred. A three-seed pilot in S2~W5 revealed high reward variance ($\sigma\approx4\times10^5$, roughly 7\% of mean), which required expanding to twenty seeds per condition (thirty at $N=40$). This caused a two-week evaluation delay (recorded in Figure~\ref{fig:gantt}) and prompted restricting the grid-size boundary sweep to $N=20$. Separately, an SF-Aware Greedy baseline was added in S2~W4 specifically to isolate long-horizon planning gain from protocol awareness, a distinction the Nearest-Sensor baseline alone could not provide.

Training divergence on large grids (reward collapse and unbounded TD loss) was the main technical risk that materialised. It was resolved by gradient clipping (max norm~10) and cutting the learning rate from $10^{-3}$ to $3\times10^{-4}$, costing about one week. This matched Risk~R3 in Appendix~\ref{app:risk}, where hyperparameter adjustment was pre-identified as the mitigation; the contingency budget absorbed the overrun without affecting the submission date. Evaluation time overrun was handled by restricting the grid-size sweep, consistent with Risk~R4. The other listed risks did not materialise.

CPD covered LoRaWAN/RF physics, DRL theory, and professional skills (Lectures~2--4; malpractice training S1~W12); full entries are in Appendix~\ref{app:cpd}. The main practical lesson from the project was that pilot-scale variance estimates before committing to a full evaluation design are worth the time; without the three-seed pilot, the seed-count expansion would have caused a much larger overrun. Baseline design deserves the same scrutiny as the primary method. All six objectives were met, with two additional contributions coming out of evaluation.
