\subsection{Summary}

This chapter has presented the complete protocol-aware DRL framework. The system model
captures the UAV's kinematics, energy consumption, and the stochastic LoRaWAN channel via the
Two-Ray Ground Reflection model and Capture Effect. The MDP formulation translates the
multi-objective optimisation problem into a dense reward signal that drives DQN training
within a high-fidelity Gymnasium environment. The following chapter evaluates the agent's
performance against standard greedy baselines.
